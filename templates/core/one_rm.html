📚 Training Insights

What is 1RM?
Your One-Rep Max is the maximum weight you can lift for a single repetition with proper form. It's the gold standard for measuring absolute strength.
📐 Formula Accuracy
Formulas are most accurate for 1–10 reps. Beyond 15 reps, estimates can vary significantly. The Epley formula is most commonly used in powerlifting.
⚠️ Safety First
Always warm up properly before attempting heavy loads. Never test your true 1RM without a spotter. Use these numbers for programming, not reckless testing.
